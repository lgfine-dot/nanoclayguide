{{/* ── 1. Why We Built This ──────────────────────────────── */}}

Why We Built This

The nanoclay information landscape is fragmented between paywalled research, vendor marketing, and scattered academic papers. We built the resource we wished existed as buyers — practical, independent, and technically rigorous.

Nanoclay Guide provides the definitive free resource for engineers, formulators, and procurement teams who need to evaluate, specify, and use nanoclays in real products.

{{/* ── 2. Powered by AGCP ────────────────────────────────── */}}

Powered by AGCP

Nanoclay Guide is produced by AGCP Farmacêuticos, LDA, a Portuguese nanotechnology company with hands-on nanoclay experience across multiple domains:

Active formulator — We are currently developing nanoclay-based agriculture products, putting us face-to-face with the same supplier selection, quality control, and formulation challenges our readers deal with daily.

Future producer — With Advanced Materials Platform funding, AGCP is building capacity to produce organically modified nanoclays at commercial scale, giving us a manufacturer's perspective on processing and quality benchmarks.

Pharma exploration — We are exploring nanoclay applications in pharmaceutical drug delivery, where clay purity, characterization, and regulatory compliance are non-negotiable.
